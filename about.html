About the Company

Trusted Since
1992

INTEDECO Nigeria Limited is a professional engineering and oil servicing company incorporated on the 4th of June 1992, with its headquarters in Port Harcourt, Rivers State, Nigeria.

In addition to our electrical and civil engineering expertise, the company has received approval from the Nigerian Department of Petroleum Resources (DPR) to render technical and engineering services in the oil and gas sector.

INTEDECO and her associates offer a wide range of upstream services including mechanical, electrical instrumentation, project management, procurement, and provision of skilled engineering personnel. Our strength lies in an admirable crop of engineers, technicians, site supervisors, and professional contract staff.
